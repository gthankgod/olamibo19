We never
planned it

A journey of a thousand miles they say begins with a step. The first step in our journey started in a classroom - Physiotherapy post graduate seminar room to be precise

It was an EMS 727 (Biostatics) tutorial lecture and it was the first time we were in the same room together, though we had stumbled on each other a few times in the past. Fast forward many steps thereafter, we had a pseudo introduction by a mutual friend, then came the friendship, dating, courting, proposal..... and now, here we are!

It has been a journey full of peace, laughter, joy, grace, growth, learning and God's faithfulness. We want to use this medium to invite you to come celebrate with us. We pray that as you do, your homes will continually be filled with God's blessings. Thank you and God bless you all.
